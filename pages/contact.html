Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We primarily service the Greater Los Angeles area from our Silver Lake studio location, but we're happy to travel to surrounding counties for your special event.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for peak season events (May-September) and 1-2 months for off-season events.

What's included in your packages?

Our packages include professional equipment, unlimited prints, digital copies, props, and a dedicated attendant. Visit our services page for detailed package information.
